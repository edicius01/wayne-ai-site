The Speed Problem in HVAC Is Getting Worse

HVAC is one of the most time-sensitive service businesses there is. When a unit fails in the heat of summer or cold of winter, customers aren't comparison shopping — they're calling until someone responds. The first company to respond wins the job. Full stop.

The data backs this up:

  • Leads contacted within 5 minutes are 9x more likely to convert than those contacted after 30 minutes
  • Over 60% of service calls go to the first company that responds
  • After-hours and weekend calls — peak failure times — are the hardest to answer and the easiest to lose

Most HVAC companies are losing a significant chunk of their potential revenue not because they do bad work, but because they can't physically answer every call the moment it comes in.
